Expected Total Bracket Points

Top numbers indicate the expected points you'll earn for each pick under a 64 points per round scoring system, excluding rounds of 64 and 32, using Vegas odds from several Vegas bookmakers to determine likelihood of outcomes.

BracketWise Score

Bottom numbers indicate the difference between likely outcomes and popular bracket choices. Look for high positive values indicating undervalued opportunitites. Avoid selections with low or negative values.

Size of Pool is Critical

You want both numbers to be high.
The size of pool determines which number you emphasize.

Emphasize Expected Total Bracket Points (top) for small brackets.
Emphasize BracketWise Score (bottom) for large brackets.
